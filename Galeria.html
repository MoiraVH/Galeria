THIS IS THE TITLE OF THE GALLERY

My friends at my middle school started talking about how babies were made, but it seemed too weird and gross to believe, so I asked my Mom how I was made as soon as I got home.

She smiled and took me down to the basement, where she showed me the tanks in the laboratory where she had grown me and my three identical sisters.
